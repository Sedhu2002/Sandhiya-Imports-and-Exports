Rajabhogam Ponni Rice – Premium, Aromatic & Reliable

Rajabhogam Ponni rice is a premium, medium-grain rice variety cultivated in southern India, known for its soft texture, delicate taste, and naturally aromatic profile.

The grains cook into fluffy, non-sticky rice that pairs perfectly with curries, sambar, and a wide range of traditional and international dishes, making it ideal for both everyday cooking and food service requirements.

  • Medium-grain parboiled Ponni rice with balanced texture and flavor.
  • Stable grain quality throughout the year for consistent cooking performance.
  • Low glycemic index, making it suitable for health-conscious consumers.
  • Carefully cleaned, processed, and packed for export-ready shipments.
